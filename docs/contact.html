Frequently Asked Questions

How do I access my purchased plan?
After payment, your account is activated immediately. Log in at login.html with the email you used at checkout.

What's the difference between Yearly and Lifetime?
Yearly gives you access to any 1 certification for 12 months. Lifetime gives you all 12 certifications forever, plus the AI Coach (Professor Cloud).

Can I switch certifications?
Yearly subscribers can contact support to switch their active certification. Lifetime subscribers have all 12 certs unlocked simultaneously.

Is there a refund policy?
Yes — if you're not satisfied within 7 days of purchase, email us and we'll process a full refund, no questions asked.

Response Times

We typically respond to all support emails within 24 hours on business days. For urgent account issues, mention "URGENT" in your subject line and we'll prioritize your request.
