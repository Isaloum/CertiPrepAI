# Product Strategy — AWSPrepAI
**Date:** 2026-03-31

---

## Problem
AWS certification candidates have limited, expensive, or poorly designed study tools. Existing platforms (Udemy, A Cloud Guru, Whizlabs) are bloated and generic. There is room for a focused, affordable, high-quality practice exam tool.

## ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)
- Developers / IT professionals studying for AWS certs
- Most common: Solutions Architect Associate, Developer Associate, SysOps
- Age 25–45, technically literate, self-directed learner
- Motivated by career advancement or job requirement
- Will pay $10–$30/month if the product saves them time and feels high quality

## Jobs to Be Done
1. **Practice under exam conditions** — timed questions, realistic format
2. **Understand why answers are right/wrong** — not just pass/fail
3. **Track progress** — know which domains are weak
4. **Study efficiently** — not spend hours on content they already know

## Differentiation
| Competitor | Weakness | Our Edge |
|------------|----------|----------|
| Udemy practice tests | Static, no explanations, outdated | Fresh questions, AI explanations |
| A Cloud Guru | Expensive ($49/month), full courses | Focused on practice only, cheaper |
| Whizlabs | Outdated UI, poor mobile | Clean UI, fast, modern |

## Value Proposition
"The fastest way to get exam-ready for AWS certifications — focused practice, instant feedback, no fluff."

## Business Model
- **Free tier:** Limited questions per month (to validate, hook users)
- **Pro tier:** Unlimited questions, all certifications, progress tracking
- **Price:** ~$9–$19/month (TBD based on validation)
- **Payment:** Stripe (already integrated)
