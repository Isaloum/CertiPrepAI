# Product Strategy — AWSPrepAI
**Date:** 2026-03-31

---

## Problem
AWS cert prep is expensive, fragmented, and outdated. Candidates pay $30+/month for tools that haven't been updated since 2023.

## ICP
Self-taught developers and cloud engineers preparing for their first or second AWS certification. Budget-conscious. Studies evenings and weekends. Wants to pass, not just practice.

## Jobs To Be Done
1. "I need to practice real exam-style questions without paying $30/month"
2. "I need to know which topics I'm weak on before exam day"
3. "I want to understand WHY an answer is correct, not just memorize"

## Differentiation
| Feature | AWSPrepAI | Competitors |
|---|---|---|
| Price | $7/month | $25–35/month |
| AI explanations | ✅ | ❌ most |
| Free tier (real questions) | ✅ SAA-C03 5Q | ❌ |
| 12 cert coverage | ✅ | ✅ |
| Built on AWS (meta-learning) | ✅ | ❌ |

## Value Proposition
The most affordable AWS cert prep platform, built on AWS itself — so you're learning the stack while you study it.

## Revenue Model
- Monthly: $7/month (1 cert)
- Yearly: discounted annual (TBD price)
- Lifetime: one-time bundle (TBD price)
- Free tier: SAA-C03 limited to 5 questions → upsell wall
